welcome to GEOMAXGIS

The GeomaxGIS Company is an industry-leading geospatial solutions provider specializing in GIS products and services to meet the diverse needs of local and international industries. GeomaxGIS’s offerings include cadastral mapping and land records management software and solutions, photogrammetric products and services, GIS asset management solutions, GIS website development and hosting and GIS consultation. We provide all the necessary components of a successful GIS system, ensuring your increased productivity and efficiency.
